Greer, Betsy. ["Taking Back the Knit: Creating Communities via Needlecraft." (PDF - 1.4MB)](http://www.craftivism.com/archives/taking.back.the.knit.pdf) MA thesis, Goldsmiths College, University of London, 2004.
